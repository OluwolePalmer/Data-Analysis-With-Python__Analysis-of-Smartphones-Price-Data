This dataset provides a comprehensive collection of information on various smartphones, enabling a detailed analysis of their specifications and pricing. It encompasses a wide range of smartphones, encompassing diverse brands, models, and configurations, making it a valuable resource for researchers, data analysts, and machine learning enthusiasts interested in the smartphone industry. The data comes from the spanish website PC componentes.
